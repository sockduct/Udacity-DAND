# Review Guide
* Look at the following Tableau Story:  [US Flight Stats](https://public.tableau.com/profile/james.small#!/vizhome/USFlightStats/USFlightsStory)
* Consider the following:
  * Is it clear to you what is being presented?
  * Are there questions you feel are not answered?
  * Do you have any suggestions for improvement?
  * Is there anything you don't like?
* Other aspects to consider:
  * What do you notice in the visualization?
  * What questions do you have about the data?
  * What relationships do you notice?
  * What do you think is the main takeaway from this visualization?
  * Is there something you don’t understand in the graphic?
